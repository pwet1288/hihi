The Girl in my Dreams.

by Vince Ryelie O. Agreda
ICT CP-D 12-Neapolis

For me, dreams were always just dreams. Nothing to look forward to, nothing too serious.
It was never a thing I could believe in, maybe it was just a funny story to tell someone
but it's never believable. Though, there was a time that I hoped this one dream came true.
A girl in my dreams appeared, she was funny, weird, beautiful in her own way. I fell for her,
my heart yearned for her, I wanted to be with her. But, I still didn't believe that it was true,
that it meant anything to me. But it gave me feeling of uncertainty.

Then, this mystery girl would soon unravel. Growing up, it became clearer, I came to realize that
my dream actually came true. For years on end I didn't expect that I would ever know who she is,
or even know what she looks like, I thought it would stay as a dream. Until she came. Then,
something changed. Or rather someone came into my life and changed everything.

Phoebe. The girl I dreamt about. She's kind, beautiful inside and out, funny, and the person
I connect with on the highest level. Talking to her, I already felt familiar, close.
It's like I had known her all my life

Phoebe illuminated the places I never realized were dark, fixed the parts of me that I never knew was broken.
She made me grow as a person, supported me, stood beside me whatever happened. I always believed I was fine with myself,
until she came, and showed me how powerful I can be with her.

Being with her, it made me scared, nervous and frightened of losing someone.
Something I thought I long accepted. Caring for someone, it makes this feeling stronger.
It makes their absence unimaginable.

This isn't only a love story, or how much I love someone. It's a realization that dreams do can come true.
And when it does, it comes with lessons, challenges, and growth you never saw coming.

I wrote this not just an expression of my love, but to show appreciation for everything Phoebe has done for me,
for our relationship.No words, or actions can ever make up to show how much I'm grateful, but I'll say this:

Phoebe, I can never express how thankful I am for you, but thank you for making my dream come true.
For being with me. For making me believe that love is more than just a simple words.
And, I love you. More than I ever thought I could love anyone.

(Me and Phoebe.)
